We formalize an executable rational canonical form for finite square matrices
over $\Q$ in Lean 4.  The construction computes the polynomial presentation
$XI-A$ with the certified Smith-normal-form kernel from
\code{lean-normal-forms}, proves that presentation equal to mathlib's
\code{Matrix.charmatrix}, and transports the complete normalized Smith
diagonal to standard polynomials.  We derive a cyclic
$\Q[X]$-module decomposition, prove that the product of all invariant factors
is the characteristic polynomial and that the last factor is the monic
minimal polynomial, and construct an explicit basis whose matrix is a
reindexed block diagonal of companion matrices.  The result carries a chosen
change-of-basis inverse and both inverse identities.  A separate similarity
certificate proves exact factor and canonical-block invariance, yielding
basis-independent statements for abstract endomorphisms.  Native execution,
raw benchmark data, API checks, and an axiom audit are packaged in an
independent artifact without enlarging the stable HNF/SNF core facade.
